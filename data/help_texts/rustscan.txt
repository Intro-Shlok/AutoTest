Usage: rustscan [OPTIONS] --address <ADDRESS>...

OPTIONS:
  -a, --address <ADDRESS>...    A comma delimited list of ip addresses and ranges to scan
  -p, --ports <PORTS>           A comma delimited list of ports to scan (default: 1-65535)
  -b, --batch <BATCH>           The batch size for port scanning, increases (default: 4500)
  -t, --timeout <TIMEOUT>       The time in milliseconds before a timeout occurs (default: 1500)
  -n, --no-nmap                 Don't run Nmap after scan
  -g, --greppable               Greppable output
  -u, --udp                     UDP scan (experimental)
      --accessible              Only show accessible ports
      --range <RANGE>           CIDR range to scan
      --tries <TRIES>           Number of tries before abandon (default: 1)
  -h, --help                    Print help information
  -V, --version                 Print version information
